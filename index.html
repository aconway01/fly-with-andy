Discovery Flights

A Discovery Flight is the best way to see what flying is really like. It's a relaxed, hands-on introduction where you'll sit up front, take the controls, and get a feel for how real flight training works — all with calm, structured guidance.

We'll begin with a short ground briefing covering the basics of flight, the aircraft, and what to expect. Then we'll head out for a one-hour introductory lesson departing from KMRB, where you'll handle the controls and experience the fundamentals firsthand. No prior experience needed.

I teach in a well-maintained Piper PA-28, and Discovery Flights are a flat $200 — including both ground instruction and flight time. Weekend availability fills quickly, so scheduling early is always a good idea.

About Me

I'm a CFI, CFII, and MEI with a calm, methodical approach to flight training. My focus is on disciplined fundamentals, sound aeronautical decision-making, and helping students build the confidence and capability needed for real-world flying.

Before becoming an instructor, I worked as a state regulator in the horse racing industry — an environment built on strict safety standards, operational oversight, and disciplined compliance. That background directly shapes how I structure training, manage risk, and maintain a professional learning environment.

I train exclusively at KMRB, a deliberate choice that gives my students a towered, low-congestion environment ideal for efficient lessons and real-world ATC communication. My instructional style emphasizes clarity, structure, and steady progression from day one.

Whether you're pursuing aviation as a career or flying for personal enjoyment, my goal is to provide a supportive, professional training experience that builds long-term proficiency — not just checkride readiness.
